Mutton Rogan Josh

Description

Mutton Rogan Josh is a tasty dish from Kashmir, a region in northern India. It features tender pieces of mutton (goat meat) cooked in a rich and aromatic gravy. The gravy is flavored with spices like cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon, giving it a warm and fragrant taste. Mutton Rogan Josh is known for its vibrant red color, which comes from the use of Kashmiri red chili powder. Whether enjoyed with rice or bread, this flavorful dish is a favorite among meat lovers for its bold flavors and comforting warmth.

Ingredients

  • Mutton (goat meat): Cut into pieces, usually bone-in for added flavor.
  • Yogurt: Used for marinating the meat and adding richness to the gravy.
  • Onions: Finely chopped or sliced, for the base of the gravy.
  • Tomatoes: Pureed or finely chopped, to add tanginess and depth to the dish.
  • Aromatic spices: Including cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, and bay leaves, for flavoring the gravy.
  • Ginger-garlic paste: Adds a punch of flavor and aroma.
  • Kashmiri red chili powder: Gives the dish its signature vibrant red color and mild heat.
  • Ground spices: Such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, for seasoning.
  • Ghee or oil: Used for cooking and flavoring the dish.
  • Fresh cilantro: Chopped for garnishing and added freshness

Steps

  1. Marinate the Mutton:
    1. In a large bowl, combine the mutton pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, Kashmiri red chili powder, and a pinch of salt.
    2. Mix well to ensure the mutton is evenly coated in the marinade.
    3. Let the mutton marinate for at least 1 hour, or overnight in the refrigerator for best results.
  2. Prepare the Gravy Base:
    1. Heat ghee or oil in a heavy-bottomed pan or pressure cooker over medium heat.
    2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown and caramelized.
    3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a few more minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add Spices:
    1. Add whole spices like cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, and bay leaves to the pan.
    2. Stir in ground spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala.
    3. Cook the spices with the onion mixture for a minute or two to release their flavors.
  4. Cook the Mutton:
    1. Add the marinated mutton to the pan, along with any remaining marinade.
    2. Cook the mutton for a few minutes until it starts to brown slightly.
  5. Simmer with Tomatoes:
    1. Pour in the pureed or finely chopped tomatoes, along with a splash of water if needed to deglaze the pan.
    2. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
    3. Cover the pan and let the mutton simmer over low heat until it is tender and cooked through. This may take about 1 to 1.5 hours, depending on the size of the mutton pieces.
  6. Adjust Seasoning and Serve:
    1. Taste the gravy and adjust the seasoning as needed with salt and Kashmiri red chili powder.
    2. Once the mutton is cooked to your liking and the gravy has thickened, remove the pan from heat.
    3. Garnish with chopped cilantro before serving.
    4. Mutton Rogan Josh is traditionally served hot with rice or naan bread.
